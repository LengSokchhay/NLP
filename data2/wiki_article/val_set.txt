History The Institute was founded in 1964 as Institut Technique Supérieur de l'Amitié Khméro–Soviétique .. Scholarships are offered to students for financial need and for merit.. In 2017, its second campus was opened in Tboung Khmum Province.. École Polytechnique of Paris is among the stronger partners with ITC, and each year several ITC students have been recruited to study with them..
